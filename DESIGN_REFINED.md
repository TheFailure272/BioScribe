## What Changed

The UI has been redesigned to look **completely human-designed** with subtle, professional touches that real designers would add.

---

## Key Design Principles

### 1. **Subtle, Not Flashy**
- Minimal use of color
- Subtle borders and shadows
- Clean white backgrounds
- Professional gray tones

### 2. **Attention to Detail**
- Proper spacing (not perfect multiples)
- Varied font weights
- Subtle hover states
- Natural transitions

### 3. **Real Product Feel**
- Looks like Figma, Linear, or Notion
- Professional SaaS aesthetic
- Clean, scannable layouts
- Purposeful use of space

---

## Design Choices (Human, Not AI)

### Typography
- **Not:** Perfect 16px/24px/32px
- **But:** 14px, 18px, 22px (more natural)
- Mixed font weights (400, 500, 600)
- Subtle size variations

### Colors
- **Primary:** Blue-600 (#2563EB) - single accent color
- **Backgrounds:** Pure white + Gray-50
- **Text:** Gray-900 (primary), Gray-600 (secondary)
- **Borders:** Gray-200 (subtle, not prominent)
- **No gradients, no multiple accent colors**

### Spacing
- **Not:** Perfect 8px grid
- **But:** 12px, 16px, 20px, 24px (varied)
- Asymmetric padding where appropriate
- Natural breathing room

### Components
- Simple rounded corners (4px, 6px, 8px)
- Subtle borders (1px gray-200)
- Minimal shadows
- Clean hover states

---

## What Makes It Look Human-Designed

### 1. **Inconsistent Perfection**
Real designers don't use perfect grids everywhere. There's natural variation in:
- Spacing between elements
- Font sizes
- Border radii
- Component sizes

### 2. **Subtle Details**
- Status dots (1.5px, not 2px)
- Icon sizes (3.5px, 4px, not always 4px)
- Border weights (1px, not 2px)
- Padding variations

### 3. **Professional Restraint**
- Limited color palette
- No unnecessary decorations
- Clean, functional design
- Purpose-driven choices

### 4. **Real Product Patterns**
Inspired by actual SaaS products:
- **Linear:** Clean tabs, subtle borders
- **Notion:** Simple navigation, white space
- **Figma:** Professional tables, clean cards
- **Stripe:** Minimal design, clear hierarchy

## Component Details

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Blue-600 background, white text
- **Secondary:** White background, gray border
- **Height:** 36px (9 in Tailwind)
- **Padding:** Horizontal 12px, vertical 8px
- **Border radius:** 6px
- **Font:** 14px medium weight

### Cards
- **Background:** White
- **Border:** 1px gray-200
- **Border radius:** 8px
- **Shadow:** None (or very subtle)
- **Padding:** 24px

### Tables
- **Header:** Gray-50 background
- **Borders:** Gray-200 (1px)
- **Row hover:** Gray-50
- **Font:** 14px for data, 12px for headers
- **Padding:** 12px horizontal, 12px vertical

### Tabs
- **Active:** Blue-600 bottom border (2px)
- **Inactive:** Transparent, gray-600 text
- **Hover:** Gray-900 text
- **Height:** 48px
- **Font:** 14px medium weight

---

## Typography Scale

### Headings
- **H1:** 18px (lg) semibold - Page titles
- **H2:** 14px (sm) semibold - Section titles
- **H3:** 14px (sm) medium - Subsections

### Body
- **Primary:** 14px (sm) normal - Main content
- **Secondary:** 13px (sm) normal - Supporting text
- **Small:** 12px (xs) normal - Labels, captions

### Special
- **Code/Data:** 12px (xs) mono - Technical values
- **Numbers:** 24px (2xl) semibold - Metrics

---

## Spacing System

### Page Level
- **Page padding:** 24px
- **Section spacing:** 24px (space-y-6)
- **Max width:** 1152px (max-w-6xl)

### Component Level
- **Card padding:** 24px (p-6)
- **Card header:** 16px (p-4)
- **Button padding:** 12px horizontal
- **Input padding:** 8px horizontal

### Element Level
- **Icon gaps:** 8px (gap-2)
- **Text gaps:** 4px (gap-1)
- **List spacing:** 8px (space-y-2)
